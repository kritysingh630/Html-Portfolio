My Top 3 Movies Of All Time

Dear Zindagi

It's a beautiful movie about a girl overcoming depression and breaking people's mindset of a taboo in India that is to not go to therapy as they think of this as a "pagal/dimaag ka doctor" and finally getting to enjoy the little and big things in life.I love this movie because of how such a big problem among people nowadays struggling with depression is portrayed in a good way.

Howl's Moving Castle

I love the animation and the simplicity of the ghibli.

Rush Hour 1 2 3

This movie has action and a lot of humor.It's a feel good movie.
